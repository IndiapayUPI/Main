Ilm-e-Ilahi aur Science

Duniya mein har cheez us Ek Khaliq ki nishani hai. Quran-e-Pak mein bar-bar irshad hua—"Afala Tatafakkurun" (Kya tum gaur nahi karte?). Aaj ki jadid (modern) science unhi raazon ko khol rahi hai jo sadiyon pehle azim Bharat ki sarzameen par 'Veda' aur 'Puran' ki surat mein nazil hue.

Ye koi mazhabi tabdili nahi, balki us purane 'Ilm-e-Ladunni' (Divine Knowledge) ki taraf wapsi hai jo insaniyat ki mushtarka (common) wirasat hai.

Muzahira-e-Qudrat (Scientific Evidences)

Makhlooqat ki Ginti (Species) Science kehti hai 8.7 Million. Sanatan Puranon mein '84 Lakh' ka zikr sadiyon pehle se hai.
Kainati Waqt (Cosmic Time) NASA ke mutabiq Suraj ki umar 4.5bn saal hai. Brahm-Vigyan mein ye ginti 4.32bn hai. Hairat-angez sateekta (accuracy)!
Zaban ka Mojza (Sanskrit) NASA ke scientist Rick Briggs ke mutabiq Sanskrit duniya ki wahid 'Logical' zaban hai jo AI ke liye sabse behtar hai.

Wahdat-ul-Wajood: Ek Hi Noor

Aksar log 'Bhagwan' aur 'Allah' ke naam par bat jate hain. Magar Upanishad kehte hain: "Ekam Sat Vipra Bahudha Vadanti"—Haqiqat ek hi hai, aqalmand log use alag-alag naamon se pukarte hain.

Jis tarah 'Aab', 'Pani' aur 'Water' kehne se cheez nahi badalti, usi tarah us Noor-e-Azam ki sifaat (qualities) ko alag roop mein dekhna shirk nahi, balki uski azmat ko pehchanna hai. Aham Brahmasmi ka matlab wahi 'Anal-Haq' hai jo Sufiya-e-Ikram ne bayan kiya.
