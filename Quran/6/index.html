🧬 84 Lakh Yoniyan (The 8.7M Mystery)

Science ne 2011-2023 ke beech prove kiya ki Earth par 8.7 Million species hain. Sanatan ne hazaron saal pehle bina kisi microscope ke 84 Lakh (8.4 Million) yoniyan batayi thin. Sirf 3% ka farq—Ye calculation kisi insani dimaag ki nahi ho sakti!

🌌 Cosmic Time (Suraj ki Umar)

NASA kehti hai Suraj 4.567 Billion saal purana hai. Puranas ke hisab se ye 4.563 Billion hai. Itni sateek (precise) ginti! Jab poori duniya andhere mein thi, tab Bharat ke paas Space Science ka pura data tha.
