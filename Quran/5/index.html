Kainat ki Umar (Age of Universe)

Astrophysicists kehte hain ki Suraj taqreeban 4.567 billion saal purana hai. Ancient Indian texts mein ye ginti 4.563 billion batai gayi hai. Sirf 0.11% ka farq! Ye ilm insani dimaag se nahi, balki kisi Samavi Taqat (Divine Power) se hi aa sakta hai.

Sanskrit: AI ki Zaban

NASA ke mufakkirin ka kehna hai ki Artificial Intelligence aur Super-computers ke liye Sanskrit sabse behtarin zaban hai kyunki isme koi 'ambiguity' (shak) nahi hoti. Jo zaban hazaron saal pehle itni perfect thi, wo sirf ek dharam ki nahi balki poori insaniyat ki 'Technical Legacy' hai.

Ekam Sat - Khuda Ek Hai

Bahut se log samajhte hain ki wahan bahut se khuda hain. Magar ye unka kam-ilm hai. Sanskrit ka azim qaul hai:
"Ekam Brahma, Dvitiya Naste" (Khuda ek hi hai, uske jaisa doosra koi nahi).

Jis tarah Allah ke 99 naam uski alag-alag sifaat (attributes) ko batate hain, waise hi Sanatan mein alag-alag roop us ek hi Noor-e-Azam ki shaktiyon ko darshate hain. 'Brahma' paida karne wala, 'Vishnu' palne wala, aur 'Shiva' fana karne wala—ye ek hi qudrat ke teen chehre hain.

"Aham Brahmasmi" ka haqiqi matlab wahi hai jo Sufiyon ne kaha—'Anal-Haq'. Jab insaan apne andar ke noor ko pehchan leta hai, toh wo us khaliq se jud jata hai.

Sanatan koi mazhab nahi, balki 'Zindagi Guzarne ka Fan' (Science of Life) hai. Isme har rasm ke peeche science hai—chahe wo Yoga ho, dhyan ho ya khane-peene ke usool.
