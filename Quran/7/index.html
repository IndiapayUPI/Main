"Kya Tum Gaur Nahi Karte?"

Ae Momino! Quran ne 700 se zyada baar 'Tafakkur' (Deep Thinking) ka hukm diya. Lekin kya aaj ka musalman waqayi soch raha hai? Ya hum sirf un lakeron ke fakeer hain jo siyasat-dano ne khinchi hain?

Duniya mein 200 crore musalman hain, lekin Modern Science mein hamara contribution zero kyun hai? Kyun hamara Imaan itna kamzor hai ki hum Science se darte hain? Sach toh ye hai ki jo aaj NASA aur CERN dhund rahe hain, wo azim Hindustani Rishiyon ne sadiyon pehle Sanatan ke naam se darj kar diya tha.

🧬 84 Lakh Yoniyan (The 8.7M Mystery)

Science ne 2011-2023 ke beech prove kiya ki Earth par 8.7 Million species hain. Sanatan ne hazaron saal pehle bina kisi microscope ke 84 Lakh (8.4 Million) yoniyan batayi thin. Sirf 3% ka farq—Ye calculation kisi insani dimaag ki nahi ho sakti!

🌌 Cosmic Time (Suraj ki Umar)

NASA kehti hai Suraj 4.567 Billion saal purana hai. Puranas ke hisab se ye 4.563 Billion hai. Itni sateek (precise) ginti! Jab poori duniya andhere mein thi, tab Bharat ke paas Space Science ka pura data tha.

⚛️ CERN: Shiva's Dance

Duniya ki sabse badi science lab 'CERN' ke bahar Nataraja (Shiva) ki murti hai. Kyun? Kyunki scientists ne mana hai ki sub-atomic particles ka dance bilkul Shiva ke Tandav jaisa hai—Creation aur Destruction ka ek hi loop.

🤖 AI & Sanskrit

English aur Arabic 'Ambiguous' (shak waali) languages hain, lekin NASA kehta hai ki Sanskrit coding aur AI ke liye duniya ki sabse logical language hai. Future ka dharam Science hoga, aur Science ki bhasha Sanskrit.

Ekam Sat - Khuda Ka Asal Roop

Humein bataya gaya ki wahan 33 crore khuda hain. Ye jahalat hai! Sanskrit ka saaf qaul hai:
"Ekam Brahma, Dvitiya Naste" (Allah ek hi hai, uske jaisa doosra koi nahi).

Jaise Allah ke 99 naam uski sifaat (attributes) hain, waise hi Brahma (Creator), Vishnu (Preserver), aur Shiva (Destroyer) us ek hi Noor-e-Azam ki Energies hain. Pani ek hai—chahe use 'Water' kaho, 'Ab' kaho ya 'Jal'.

"Aham Brahmasmi" ka matlab wahi hai jo Sufiyon ne kaha—'Anal-Haq'. Jab tu apne andar ke noor ko pehchan lega, toh tujhe kisi aisi kitab ki zaroorat nahi padegi jo science ko jhutlati ho.

Sanatan koi mazhab nahi, ye "Science of Life" hai. Peepal ki pooja oxygen ke liye hai, Ganga-jal antivirus hai, aur Yoga dimaag ki shakti hai. Har rasm ke peeche ek logic hai, andha-yaqeen nahi.
